About Us

Six Flags Over Texas, located in Arlington, Texas, is the original Six Flags! Opening on August 1, 1961, it is the oldest Six Flags theme park in the world. I chose this park because it's the favorite park of my wonderful (soon to be) fiancée! I also chose this park because of the AMAZING selection of rides! Contained here is just a small selection, only 6 of our favorites, but there are many more we could have highlighted!

Upcoming Events

  1. Star Spangled Nights (July 3-4) - Fireworks and patriotic entertainment celebrating America's 250th anniversary!
  2. Fright Fest presented by Snickers (September 12 - November 1) - Thrills by day, chills by night!
  3. Holiday in the Park (November 21 - December 30) - Christmas lights, holiday shows, and seasonal food and entertainment!
